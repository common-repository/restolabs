Restolabs Online Ordering Plugin generates online food ordering option which can be placed anywhere on the site.

== Description ==

Restolabs provides an online ordering solution tailored for the food industry. Whether you are a restaurant , a meal provider or a caterer , Restolabs is able to handle your ordering needs with ease.
This plugin allows site owner to easily add our ordering widget to their wordpress site.
Restolabs offers a 30 day free trial for you to explore the system and only purchase once you have evaluated the system.

More about Restolabs on [restolabs.com](http://restolabs.com/)


== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `restolabs` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Restolabs' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
